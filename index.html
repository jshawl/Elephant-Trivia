If these facts strike your fancy, I recommend checking out Elephant Nature Park. Just north of Chiang Mai in Thailand, ENP is an santuary for elephants, founded by Lek Chailert, who rescues elephants in need and provides them a safe haven where they are able to live out their lives free of any work (including tourist rides) and abuse. ENP is also home to dogs, cats and water buffalo.
